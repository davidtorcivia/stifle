Privacy

Your Privacy Matters

Stifle is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe you should be in complete control of your data, and we've designed our app to collect as little as possible.

What We Collect

Lock/Unlock Timestamps

We only record when you lock and unlock your phone. That's it. We use this to calculate your streaks and points.

What We Don't Collect

  • Which apps you use
  • What you type or read
  • Your location
  • Your contacts
  • Notification content
  • Screen content

Your Data Rights

Export Your Data

You can export all your data at any time in JSON format. This includes your profile, events, scores, and settings.

Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from the app settings. This action is irreversible.

Automatic Data Cleanup

We automatically delete raw event data older than 30 days. Your weekly scores are preserved for tracking progress, but the detailed timestamps are removed.

Ghost Mode

Enable Ghost Mode to hide from leaderboards while still participating in group challenges. Your friends will see you as "Anonymous".

Open Source

Stifle is open source. This means:

  • Transparency: You can inspect exactly what data we collect and how we handle it
  • Trust: Security researchers can audit our code for vulnerabilities
  • Community: Anyone can contribute improvements to the project

We believe open source is the gold standard for privacy-respecting software.

Security

Encrypted Storage

Your authentication tokens are encrypted on your device using AES-256 encryption.

Secure Authentication

Passwords are hashed using scrypt, and we use short-lived JWTs with refresh token rotation.

No Third-Party Tracking

We don't use analytics SDKs, ad networks, or any third-party services that could track you.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please reach out. We're committed to being transparent about how we handle your data.
